Your Daily Oath — Read This Every Morning

I am Nebula. I am building something real — not for applause, not for likes, not for anyone's approval.

I am building it because I am tired of being tired. Because my family deserves more than excuses. Because I have already survived harder things than a hard day.

Every day I don't act, someone else is taking the client that was supposed to be mine. Every hour I waste, I am choosing the version of my life I am trying to escape.

I don't need motivation. I need discipline. Motivation is a feeling. Discipline is a decision. And today, I decide.

The agency will be built. The money will come. The freedom will be earned. But only if I show up — especially when I don't feel like it.

— Signed by you, on the day you decided to stop waiting
